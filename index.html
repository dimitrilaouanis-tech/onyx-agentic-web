Why a trust layer — and why now

Every layer shipped — protocol, addressing, discovery, payments. The one missing piece is a neutral check between an agent and reality.

Conflict-free or nothing

Trust layers are always captured by the party with no stake in the outcome. Marketplaces can't grade their own GMV. Onyx grades nobody it profits from.

June 2026 is pre-SSL, pre-Chainlink for AI. The standardizing crisis hasn't hit yet. Build the check before it's mandatory.
